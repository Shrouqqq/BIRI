What is Biri?

When we conducted research, we found that we can recycle eggshells and extract products from them that we can benifit from.

Why did we make a Biri?

We created an eggshell project to help people with special needs,In order to provide products to the Egyptian market at a competitive price.

What did we get from Biri project?

Collagen, Calcium Chloride and Hyaluronic acid.

Benefits of Biri?

We help companies provide useful products, we produce natural products that are useful to people, and we reduce the expenses incurred when importing products from abroad We provide people with special needs with job opportunities.
